URI:

The

Surgical Strike

Trailer

Uri is a 2019 Bollywood action-drama, helmed by debut Aditya Dhar. The movie is all about surgical strike carried out by Indian army on Pakistani terrorists positioned across the Line of Control. Uri stars Vicky Kaushal, Paresh Rawal, Yami Gautam, Kirti Kulhari, etc. in the lead roles. The film has been divided into chapters which ultimately culminates in the surgical strikes. Major Vihaan Singh Shergill (Vicky Kaushal) is a valiant army man who aces in strategic operations. However, when his mother's (Swaroop Sampat) Alzheimer starts progessing rapidly, he bids goodbye to his border adventures and requests for a transfer to an army base in the Capital so that he can spend some time with her. Unfortunately during that period, Vihaan's brother-in-law Karan (Mohit Raina) who is also his fellow army-man gets killed in the Uri attack. This incident triggers Vihaan to join the surgical strike operation and go all guns blazing to avenge the loss of his slained fellow officers in the Uri attacks. Uri is produced by Ronnie Screwvala under the banner RSVP Movies Pvt. Ltd. Mitesh Mirchandani has wielded the camera for Uri.
